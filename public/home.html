Rules

How to Play

01
🃏
Cards are dealt
Each player gets a hand of alien cards. Each card has 6 stats: Height, Power, Speed, Strength, Intelligence, Durability.
02
📐
First player picks a stat
The player whose turn it is chooses which stat to compete on. Everyone plays their current card on that same stat.
03
🏆
Highest stat wins the trick
The player with the highest value in the chosen stat wins all played cards — stacking them onto their deck.
04
👑
Winner picks next stat
The trick winner now chooses the stat for the next round. Strategy matters — pick the stat your card excels at!
05
💀
Lose all cards? You're out
Cards are eliminated when they run out. You can stay and spectate, or head home. Last player with cards wins!
06
⚡
Auto-play if AFK
After the stat is set, all players have 5 seconds to play. Idle players are auto-played so the game never stalls.
